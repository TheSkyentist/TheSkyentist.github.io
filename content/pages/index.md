research_blurb: I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Max-Planck Institute for Astronomy. I work as a member of the RUBIES survey studying Little Red Dots with JWST NIRSpec. In particular, I develop <a href="https://github.com/TheSkyentist/unite" target="_blank"><code>unite</code></a>, an accelerated framework for fitting emission lines across multiple dispersers simultaneously. In addition, I am a member of the NIRISS WFSS pure parallel survey, OutThere, where I develop an end to end <a href="https://github.com/OutThere-JWST/NISPureParallel" target="_blank">pipeline</a> for the reduction and analysis of slitless spectroscopic data.
