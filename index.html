Astronomy Research

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Max-Planck Institute for Astronomy. I work as a member of the RUBIES survey studying Little Red Dots with JWST NIRSpec. In particular, I develop unite, an accelerated framework for fitting emission lines across multiple dispersers simultaneously. In addition, I am a member of the NIRISS WFSS pure parallel survey, OutThere, where I develop an end to end pipeline for the reduction and analysis of slitless spectroscopic data.

As a member of the data science department I routinely teach workshops on modern astronomical computing and methods. I am passionate about mentoring students and engaging in public outreach to educate the next generation of astronomers. If you'd like me to speak at your organization or event, please get in touch with me below.
